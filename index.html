About Me

I am a Thermal/Hydraulic Nuclear Engineer at Naval Nuclear Laboratory. I model fluid and thermal behavior in nuclear reactors and analyze thermal effects to optimize reactor performance.

I'm passionate about solving thermal engineering problems with applications in clean energy and aerodynamics. My experience spans nuclear engineering, thermal analysis, and computational modeling, with a strong foundation in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and heat/mass transfer.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a B.S.E. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Pennsylvania in 2025. I concentrated in Energy, Fluids, & Thermal Systems and minored in Mathematics. Heat Transfer Modeling Package

This Python package provides comprehensive thermal modeling capabilities for steady-state and transient thermal analysis, with specialized capabilities for modeling ablative materials used in spacecraft heat shields. Features include 1D/2D steady-state and transient solvers, 3D cylindrical heat shield simulations, and advanced ablation physics with material sublimation modeling.

Finite Wing Aerodynamics

MATLAB implementation of panel method solver for finite wing aerodynamics analysis. Features include lift distribution calculations, induced velocity analysis, and propeller-wing interaction modeling. The solver uses iterative vortex panel methods to compute circulation distributions and quantify the effects of propeller slipstream on wing performance.

GitHub Details
MATLAB Panel Methods Aerodynamics Vortex Methods Propeller Theory

2D CAM Software

Educational 2D CAM software that enables students to draw designs on a computer and immediately cut them on a CNC machine. Features automated draw-to-GCode conversion and intelligent auto Z-axis homing for safe and accurate machining operations. The system streamlines the design-to-manufacturing workflow, making CNC machining more accessible in educational environments.
